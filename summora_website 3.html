Modern Sumsum
Delights

Summora was born from a single belief: traditional Indonesian flavors deserve a modern stage. We take sumsum, the beloved rice flour porridge, and reinvent it with premium toppings, house-made sauces, and meticulous craft.

From silky taro ice cream and chewy boba pearls to vibrant mango chunks and golden caramel drizzles, each bowl is a bridge between heritage and contemporary taste.
